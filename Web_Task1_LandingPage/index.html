Our Sweet Little Story

CocoaCharm began as a weekend hobby when we started gifting homemade chocolates to friends and family. The response was so sweet (pun intended) that we decided to turn it into a proper small business. Today we still make everything by hand, in small batches, with the same love as our very first box.

No preservatives, no shortcuts, just good chocolate made with care.
